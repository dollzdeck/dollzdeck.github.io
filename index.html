The Lore

What is Dollz // Deck?

A trading card collection stuck between a dial-up modem and a holographic sticker sheet.

Dollz // Deck is a 120-piece holographic trading card collection pulling from the same DNA as your favorite Y2K internet dolls — oekaki sketch linework, CRT scanlines, and the collector-card format of a real TCG pack.

Every doll carries five stat lines (dreaminess, sensitivity, mystery, style, chaos), a special ability, and a core style pulled from five families: Lolita, Harajuku, Gyaru, Hypebeast, and Prep. Rarity runs five tiers deep, from Common static to true Secret Rare holo.

No two dollz share a hairstyle, outfit, or sidebar charm set — every card is a one-of-one combination, verifiable on-chain.
